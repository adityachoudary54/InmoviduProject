About DTU

Delhi Technological University (DTU), formerly known as the Delhi College of Engineering (DCE), is a public university located in New Delhi, India. It was established in 1941 as Delhi Polytechnic under the control of the Government of India. It is Delhi's first Engineering college and amongst the few engineering institutions in India set up prior to independence.
